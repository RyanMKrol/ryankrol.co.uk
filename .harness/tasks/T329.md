## Overview
The 'Session volume' chart on individual exercise pages (src/components/ExerciseProgressCharts.js) currently renders as a bar chart with many thin bars packed across months of session history, which reads as cluttered — replace it with a line chart, matching the visual style (colored line + gradient-filled area under it) already used by the two sibling charts on the same page ('Estimated 1RM progress' and 'Max weight progress').

## Do
Reshape `volumeChartData` in src/components/ExerciseProgressCharts.js from its current Bar-chart shape into the same Line-chart shape used by `oneRMChartData`/`maxWeightChartData`: `borderColor: chartIndigo`, `backgroundColor: gradientFill(chartIndigo)`, `borderWidth: 2`, and `fill: true`, dropping the old per-point `backgroundColor` array and `borderRadius` entirely — a flat single-color line with no special last-point treatment (owner confirmed: fine to lose the 'highlight the most recent session' behavior, match the siblings exactly). Swap the `<Bar data={volumeChartData} .../>` element for `<Line data={volumeChartData} .../>` in the 'Session volume' card. Remove the `Bar` import (from 'react-chartjs-2') and the `BarElement` import + `ChartJS.register(...)` entry (from 'chart.js') at the top of the file if nothing else in it still uses them after this change. Scope is just this chart-shape conversion — no axis/tooltip formatting or insights-text changes. Do not touch src/components/ProgrammeOverviewCharts.js — its Bar chart is a separate, intentionally-aggregated (category-scale, frequency-by-month) chart on the /workouts page, explicitly out of scope for this task.
