## Overview
Human decision point: compare the perfume card layout variants built in the preceding task and decide which (if any) should become the live card.

## Do
Run `npm run dev` and open `/dev/perfume-card-variants` (built by the dependency task). Compare the rendered variants — differing in rating-number size/position (enlarged in place vs. moved into the header next to the title) and 'Best for' season-chip position (current position under the rating row vs. relocated lower on the card, e.g. after the longevity/projection scales) — against real perfume data. Decide which variant (if any) should become the live `/reviews/perfumes` card, including whether the winning combination mixes elements from more than one shown variant (e.g. 'header rating' + 'Best-for at bottom' together), or whether none of the options are good enough and the card should stay as-is (a legitimate outcome, as happened before on this repo's T092 variant sweep).

## Done when
A decision is recorded in `.harness/designs/perfume-card-redesign-options-decision.md` (create it) stating either which variant (or combination of variant elements) won and a one-line reason, or that none were good enough to promote and why. This task is marked done via `.harness/mark-done.sh <its-real-task-id>` (or the dashboard's 'Mark done' button) once the decision is recorded. Note: promoting the winner into the live card and deleting the losing variants/dev page (mirroring T187) is a natural follow-up but is intentionally NOT part of this task's scope — it should be authored as a separate future task once a winner is picked.
