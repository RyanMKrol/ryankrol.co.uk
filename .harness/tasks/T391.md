## Overview
Books' review card (SpineCoverCard in src/components/ReviewCard.js) already truncates a long review to 260 characters via src/lib/reviewText.js's truncateReviewText(), keeps expand/collapse state in a useState, and renders a `.spine-cover-expand-btn` 'Read more'/'Show less' toggle beneath the snippet — this exists ONLY for books today. The other two card variants rendered by the same file — SquareCoverCard (albums, styleVariant="square-cover") and the inline poster-banner block (movies + tv, styleVariant="poster-banner") — render their full review markdown with no truncation at all, which is why a single long review (e.g. the owner's real example: a very long review on the movies page for 'A Haunting in Venice') can tower over neighbouring cards and break the masonry-grid layout on /reviews/movies, /reviews/tv, and /reviews/albums. This task ports the existing, already-decided truncation pattern to those two remaining variants — it is not designing anything new.
