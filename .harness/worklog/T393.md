## Visual-harness gap found and fixed

`scripts/_visual-harness.mjs`'s `PAGES` list had **no coverage at all** for the movies/tv/books/albums/
perfumes add or edit-detail routes (only `hot-takes-add`, `hot-takes-edit-detail`, and
`vinyl-edit-detail` existed as detail-form captures). The task spec assumed re-running
`visual-check.mjs` would produce "add/edit shots for these 12 pages", but 10 of the 12 had no
manifest entry to capture. Since `scripts/` is `SCOPE_EXEMPT_GLOBS`-exempt, I added 10 new `PAGES`
entries (`reviews-{movies,tv,books,albums,perfumes}-add` and
`reviews-{movies,tv,books,albums,perfumes}-edit-detail`), each `waitFor: ['.collection-form-card']`,
pointing at fixture IDs that carry Markdown content (`id-dune`, `id-andor`, `bk-piranesi`,
`al-for-ever`, `pf-nu-e-bleue`) so the preview pane's real Markdown rendering (bold/italic/list) is
visible in the edit-detail shots, not just an empty preview on the add shots.
