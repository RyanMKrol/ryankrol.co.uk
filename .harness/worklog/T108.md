# T108 — exclude private repos from /api/github/repos

## What changed
- `src/pages/api/github/repos.js`: the `.filter()` predicate that keeps non-fork,
  pushed repos now also requires `!repo.private`, so private repos owned by the
  account (returned by the authenticated `/user/repos?affiliation=owner` endpoint
  when `GITHUB_TOKEN` is set) are excluded from `activeRepos` and never reach the
  public `/projects` page. `isPrivate: repo.private` is left in the `.map()` as
  instructed — it's now always `false` for surviving repos.
- `src/pages/api/github/repos.test.js`: added a case that seeds `global.fetch`
  with one public repo and one `private: true` repo, invokes the handler with
  `withApiCache`/`generateCacheKey` mocked to call straight through and
  `checkRateLimit` mocked to allow, and asserts the JSON response's `repos` array
  excludes the private repo, includes the public one, and `total === 1`. A
  pre-existing `repos.test.js` (rate-limit smoke tests) was extended in place
  rather than replaced.
